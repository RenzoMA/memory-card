How to Play Memory Quest

Game Instructions

Memory Quest is a fun and engaging card game designed to test and improve your memory. Here's how you can play:

  • Flip over two cards at a time and try to find a match.
  • If the cards match, they will remain flipped over.
  • If they don't match, remember what was on each card and where it was.
  • The game continues until all cards have been matched.

Remember, the faster you find all pairs, the higher your score!
